Reservation Policy and Form

At Local Craft beer & kitchen, we strive to take the stress out of planning your party or event. Our reservation management staff is excited to help you plan and host your special event, allowing you to relax and enjoy the party with confidence. We accept reservations for groups of 10 or more, only.

Reservation policy

We accept reservations for groups of 10 or more, only.
Parties of 10 or more will require reservations at least 24 hours in advance. Reservations of 15 or more will be held to a 2-hour time limit.
A valid credit card must be provided to hold the reservation.
For groups of 30 or more, please inquire about rental & package options - andrew@local.beer
